WHAT IS WEB SUMMIT?

Web Summit is one of the largest tech conference where enterprises, startups, and people who work in technology gather and connect. The week-long conference happens in Lisbon, Portugal this year, consisting 25 different sub conferences that focus on different verticals of technologies.

There are about 60k+ attendees from 160+ countries ranging from engineers, product managers, designers to tech journalists and management executives. It’s a great opportunity to learn, get inspired, and connect with interesting people.

WHY SHOULD I GO?

Talk A Lot

Out of comfort zone. Practice articulating ideas and thoughts. Crucial PM skill.

Be Connected

Make friends. Get to know interesting people. Build relationships.

Get Inspired

Absorb the latest idea and best practice from everywhere, everyone.

As someone who is very new to conference attending, I was fortunate enough to attend last year’s Tableau conference in Austin, TX. It was an eye opening experience to see how companies make use of the technology and build their analytics around the toolset. What’s more rewarding is the connections I made that allow me to seek advice after the conference. To this date we still have a highly active support group that shares the latest knowledge and best practices.

This good experience very much encouraged me to attend more when possible. In addition, I think a good PM should be able to execute and be constantly learning about what’s new in the industry. Attending tech conferences allows me to get a better grasp of the latest trends.
